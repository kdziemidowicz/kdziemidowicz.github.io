Lab head

Principal Investigator

Principal Investigator · Lecturer in Pharmaceutics
Dr Karolina Dziemidowicz

Karolina leads the group's research on electrospun biomaterials, immunostimulatory drug delivery, and localised CNS therapeutics. She completed her PhD in the CDT in Advanced Therapeutics and Nanomedicines at UCL and subsequently held an EPSRC Doctoral Prize Fellowship at the UCL Centre for Nerve Engineering.
