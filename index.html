Welcome to Dziemidowicz Lab at UCL School of Pharmacy

We are a multidisciplinary team developing novel biomaterials and drug delivery platforms

Research interests

Immunostimulatory biomaterials

Safely delivering fragile biomolecules such as antibodies, peptides or cytokines to enhance immune responses in cancer immunotherapy.

Electrohydrodynamic processes

Efficiently encapsulating small molecules and biomolecules using electrospraying and electrospinning to improve therapeutic efficacy and patient acceptability.

Localised drug delivery

Exploring innovative methods to deliver therapeutics directly to the site of action, including convection-enhanced delivery into the brain and drug-eluting implantable materials.

Protein, peptide and polymer delivery

Looking for innovative methods of delivering biomolecules using advanced drug delivery methods and formulation strategies.
