Biography

Dr Karolina Dziemidowicz is a Lecturer in Pharmaceutics at UCL School of Pharmacy and head of the Dziemidowicz Lab. Her research sits at the intersection of materials science, pharmaceutical sciences, and biomedical engineering.

Karolina completed her PhD in the CDT in Advanced Therapeutics and Nanomedicines at UCL School of Pharmacy. Following her doctorate, she was awarded an EPSRC Doctoral Prize Fellowship, hosted by the UCL Centre for Nerve Engineering, to develop drug-releasing dural substitutes for decompression neurosurgery following spinal cord injury.

She joined the UCL academic faculty and established her independent research group, working on immunostimulatory biomaterials for cancer immunotherapy, localised drug delivery to the central nervous system, and electrohydrodynamic fabrication of precision drug delivery systems.
